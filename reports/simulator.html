Interactive Pricing Simulator

Drag the levers. Conversion, margin, and the recommendation update live.

Simplified what-if model · built on the same synthetic curves

What this is. A hands-on version of the pricing analysis. You set a discount, and it shows — instantly — whether it wins more customers and whether it still makes money after the price cut.

How to use it:

  1. Pick a customer segment — different customers react differently to discounts.
  2. Drag the three sliders — discount depth, your profit margin, and average order size.
  3. Read the result — the cards, the chart, and the plain-English verdict all update as you move.

1 · Pick a customer segment

Key terms

Conversion
The customer actually places an order (not just browses).
Exposure
One chance to convert — a customer opening the app with intent to buy.
Margin
What’s left after costs — the actual profit on a sale.
Gross margin rate
Your profit as a share of the order value, before any discount.
Elastic / inelastic
Elastic = reacts strongly to price (new customers). Inelastic = barely reacts (loyal customers).
pp
Percentage points — the plain gap between two percentages (40% → 45% is +5 pp).

This simulator is a simplified interactive model built on the conversion and margin curves from the main analysis (synthetic data). It’s for exploring “what if” — the rigorous experiment reading (Simpson’s-Paradox correction, CUPED, guardrails) lives in the main dashboard. Figures are illustrative.
